LinkedIn Direct Connect

A simpler alternative approach for LinkedIn integration

Since LinkedIn OAuth is proving difficult, we can use a simpler alternative approach:

Just provide your LinkedIn profile URL directly and we'll extract what we need from it.

Example: https://www.linkedin.com/in/johndoe

Why this approach?

LinkedIn's OAuth implementation can be restrictive, especially in development environments. This alternative method allows you to continue using PrepTalk without OAuth complications.

What happens next?

The information you provide will be used in the same way as if it were retrieved via OAuth. You'll still get the full PrepTalk experience with personalized interview preparation.
